Advertising Policy

1. Overview

The CGPA & GPA Calculator website displays advertisements to support the continued operation and improvement of our free calculator tool. This Ad Policy explains how we use advertising on our website and your options regarding targeted advertising.

2. Google AdSense

2.1 What is Google AdSense?

We use Google AdSense, an advertising network managed by Google, to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense is a program that allows website publishers to earn revenue by displaying ads to their users.

ℹ️ AdSense Information: Our site is designed to meet Google AdSense policies and best practices for user experience.

2.2 How Google AdSense Works

Google AdSense analyzes the content of our website and displays ads that are relevant to your interests based on:

  • The content you are viewing on our site
  • Your browsing history and search history
  • Your previous interactions with ads
  • Demographic information (age, gender, interests)

2.3 Personalized Advertising

Google may use cookies and similar technologies to track your activity across websites and provide you with personalized advertisements. This data is used by Google and their advertising partners to:

  • Deliver relevant ads to you
  • Measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns
  • Build user profiles based on interests

3. Types of Advertisements

Our website may display various types of advertisements, including:

3.1 Display Ads

Banner advertisements displayed at the top, middle, and bottom of our website pages. These may be image-based, text-based, or animated ads.

3.2 Responsive Ads

Ads that automatically adjust their size and format based on the device and screen size you are using.

3.3 Contextual Ads

Advertisements that are relevant to the content on the page you are viewing (e.g., educational products when viewing our calculator).

3.4 Behavioral Ads

Advertisements based on your browsing history and interests as determined by Google and advertising partners.

4. Ad Placements

Advertisements appear in the following locations on our website:

  • Top banner area (above the main content)
  • Middle section (between calculator and information sections)
  • Bottom banner area (before the footer)
  • Within the sidebar or contextual areas
⚠️ Important: We strive to maintain a good balance between content and advertisements to ensure a quality user experience. All ads comply with Google AdSense policies.

5. Data Collection and Privacy

5.1 How Google Collects Data

When you visit our website, Google and its partners may collect the following data:

  • Your IP address
  • Your device type and operating system
  • Your browser type
  • Pages you visit on our site
  • Time spent on pages
  • Your approximate location
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

5.2 Google's Use of Your Data

Google uses this data to:

  • Display targeted advertisements relevant to your interests
  • Measure ad performance and user engagement
  • Provide analytics and reporting data
  • Prevent fraud and maintain security

6. Cookie Information

Cookies are small files placed on your device that help advertisers deliver relevant content. Types of cookies used:

6.1 First-Party Cookies

Cookies set by our website to remember your preferences and improve your experience.

6.2 Third-Party Cookies

Cookies set by Google and other advertising partners to track your activity and deliver personalized ads.

7. Opting Out of Personalized Advertising

You have several options to control personalized advertising:

7.1 Google Ads Settings

You can manage your Google Ads personalization preferences at: https://www.google.com/settings/ads

Here you can:

  • View the information Google has collected about you
  • Opt out of interest-based ads
  • Control which ads categories you see

7.2 Browser Settings

You can adjust your browser settings to:

  • Block cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Enable Do-Not-Track (DNT) feature

7.3 Opt-Out Tools

Various opt-out tools are available:

  • Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA) Opt-Out
  • Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) Opt-Out
  • European Interactive Digital Advertising Alliance (EDAA) Opt-Out
ℹ️ Note: Opting out does not mean you will stop seeing ads. You will still see ads, but they may be less relevant to your interests.
