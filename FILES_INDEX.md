## Quick Reference: What to Edit

### To Change Email Addresses
Find in: `index.html`, `privacy-policy.html`, `terms.html`, `ad-policy.html`
Search for: `@gpacalculator.com`
Replace with: Your email

### To Change Colors
Find in: `styles.css`
Search for: `:root {`
Change: CSS color variables

### To Add AdSense
Find in: `index.html`
Search for: `ca-pub-xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx`
Replace with: Your Publisher ID

### To Create Ad Slots
Find in: `index.html`
Search for: `data-ad-slot`
Replace with: Your slot IDs (3 places)

### To Change Calculator Settings
Find in: `script.js`
Search for: `MAX_SUBJECTS = 10`
Change to: Your desired max
